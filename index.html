Shri Shri Durga Puja Celebration Committee Successfully celebrated the Holy Durga Puja. The Ceremony commenced with the visarjan of the idols of God & Goddesses. Since its inception in 1948 ,it is an astonishing achievement of the One of the oldest Puja Celebration Committees of Danapur Cantt. The achievement is great because in spite of very less man power and drastic decrease in the collection of funds, the committee has somehow managed to celebrate in such a splendid manner.
